# Design System: Lucio Torres 2026 (Redesign) - Editorial Gentil

**Status:** Validated
**Context:** Presidential Campaign for Lucio Torres (2026).
**Core Identity:** The People's Guardian (Cercano, cálido y profesional).
**Design Style:** Editorial Gentil (Clean, prestige magazine feel).

---

## 1. Global Rules

### Color Palette (Official Colombia Colors)

| Role | Hex | Usage |
|------|-----|-------|
| **Yellow (Primary)** | `#FCD116` | Accents, highlights, secondary buttons. |
| **Blue (Secondary)** | `#003893` | Titles, navigation, authority elements. |
| **Red (Action)** | `#CE1126` | Main CTA buttons, urgent alerts. |
| **Background** | `#FDFCF8` | Warm paper/cream background. |
| **Text** | `#1A1A1A` | High contrast for readability. |
| **Border** | `#E2E8F0` | Subtle editorial lines. |

### Typography

- **Heading Font:** Newsreader (Serif)
- **Body Font:** Inter or Roboto (Sans-serif)
- **Accent Font:** Libre Baskerville (Italic for quotes/journalistic notes)
- **Google Fonts:** [Newsreader + Inter](https://fonts.google.com/share?selection.family=Newsreader:ital,opsz,wght@0,6..72,200..800;1,6..72,200..800|Inter:wght@300;400;500;600;700)

### Spacing & Grid
- **Grid:** 12-column modular grid with generous gutters.
- **Margins:** Large outer margins (10vw) for the "Editorial" feel.
- **Radii:** 4px (Soft corner) or 0px (Sharp editorial) - avoid large rounded corners.

---

## 2. Style Guidelines

- **White Space:** Massive use of negative space to allow colors to "breathe".
- **Dividers:** Use thin 1px lines in Blue or Gold.
- **Imagery:** High-quality photography with warm, natural grading.
- **Interactions:** Smooth fade-ins (300ms), no aggressive scales.

---

## 3. Anti-Patterns
- ❌ **No Clutter:** Avoid too many elements on screen.
- ❌ **No Generic Gradients:** Only solid official colors or very subtle paper textures.
- ❌ **No Emojis:** Use clean SVG icons only.
- ❌ **No Pure Black:** Use #1A1A1A for a softer feel.
